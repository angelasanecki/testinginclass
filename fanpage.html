    • Bio

    U2 was founded in 1976. Bono got the name "Bono" from an add for a hearing aid that he saw. Their music often has a poliical message. The band participates in activism and has supported Amnesty International and Greenpeace. The most recent U2 album is Songs of Innocence. You can find out more about U2 at their Wikipedia page.
